Board and Training Packages

Full Service Board
Full care board includes a 12x12 stall, daily stall cleaning, twice daily feeding of grain and supplements and hay, daily turn out (as weather permits). All stall bedding, hay, and grain is included in your board.
Partial Board
Partial Board includes a 12x12 stall, twice daily feeding of grain and supplements and hay, and daily turn out (as weather permits). All stall bedding, hay, and grain is included in your board. With partial board you are responsible for cleaning your own stalls at least once a day.
